## Clarity Beats Length

A long answer can still be weak if it hides the point. A short answer can be strong when it names the answer, gives context, and avoids unsupported claims.

## Trust Comes From Traceability

When an answer relies on facts, readers should be able to tell where those facts came from or how the author reasoned from them.
