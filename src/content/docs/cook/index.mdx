## Cheap Meals

Staple ingredients, batch cooking, leftovers, meal planning, and honest per-serving costs. Guides should count condiments, cooking fat, energy, and wasted ingredients when those costs materially change the result.

## Baking

Doughs, batters, leavening, temperature, timing, structure, and texture. Start with [How Does Baking Powder Work?](/articles/how-does-baking-powder-work/).

## Ingredient Substitutions

Explain the ingredient's job before suggesting a replacement. State how flavor, texture, rise, browning, storage life, or food safety will change.

## Kitchen Troubleshooting

Diagnose the symptom in order: ingredients, measurement, equipment, temperature, timing, and method. Separate a fast rescue from the better method for next time.

## Food Safety

Storage, cooking temperatures, cooling, reheating, cross-contamination, allergies, and spoilage require authoritative sources and conservative limits.
