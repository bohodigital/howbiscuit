How Biscuit is a static Cloudflare Pages site. It does not include accounts, comments, forms, or reader-submitted content.

## Analytics in use

We use two analytics systems to understand which guides are useful and whether the site is working properly:

- **Umami**, hosted on infrastructure operated for How Biscuit, records privacy-restrained traffic and events. The tracker is limited to `howbiscuit.com` and `www.howbiscuit.com`, honors Do Not Track, and excludes search-query values.
- **Google Analytics 4** records aggregated information about visits, devices, and interactions. Google Analytics may use browser storage and send usage information to Google under [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

We use this information to measure readership, find broken or confusing pages, and improve the publication. We do not use How Biscuit analytics to sell reader profiles.

Cloudflare and other hosting providers may also process standard request data needed to serve and protect the site. Browser privacy controls, content blockers, and Do Not Track settings may limit analytics collection.

This notice reflects the active site configuration as of July 12, 2026. Questions or privacy requests can be sent through the [contact page](/contact/).
