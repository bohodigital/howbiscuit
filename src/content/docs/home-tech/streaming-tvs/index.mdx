Streaming & TVs covers televisions, streaming boxes and sticks, game-console video, HDMI connections, soundbars, receivers, picture settings, apps, and playback problems.

## Separate the Signal Path

When picture or sound fails, trace the path in order: source device, cable, television input, television settings, audio device, and network connection. Change one part at a time. Replacing every cable or resetting every device at once makes it harder to learn which part failed.

For buffering, compare another app on the same device and the same app on another device. That distinguishes an app or service problem from a network or hardware problem.

## Before a Factory Reset

Write down account names, accessibility settings, picture adjustments, network details, and connected-device labels. A reset may remove all of them without fixing a failing cable, service outage, or account problem.
