## How Home Tech Guides Are Organized

Every article belongs to one primary section and may link to related sections. A guide about improving wireless coverage belongs in **Wi-Fi & Routers**. A guide about reducing lag on a gaming desktop belongs in **Gaming PCs**, even when the network is part of the diagnosis. A laptop buying guide belongs in **Laptops**, with a related link from Buying Guides.

This structure keeps broad searches useful without forcing readers through vague labels.

## Before You Change Anything

1. Write down the device model, operating system, app version, and exact symptom.
2. Change one setting at a time so you can undo it.
3. Save current settings or make a backup before resets, firmware changes, storage work, or account changes.
4. Do not disable security features merely because a troubleshooting page says it may make the problem disappear.
5. Check the warranty, rental agreement, or service agreement before opening equipment or altering provider-owned hardware.
