Home Tech is for everyday devices, networks, settings, repairs, and troubleshooting that a non-specialist can act on without turning the house into a lab.

Articles here should separate diagnosis from fixes, and should avoid asking readers to change security settings without a clear reason.

## Start Here

- Identify the device or setting before changing it.
- Prefer safe defaults over clever tweaks.
- Keep manufacturer steps separate from general advice.

## What This Section Covers

- home Wi-Fi and routers
- internet speed and coverage
- device settings
- basic privacy and security
