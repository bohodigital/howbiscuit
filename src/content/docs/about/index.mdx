How Biscuit publishes practical, answer-first guides for people trying to fix something, choose a product, or understand a system without wasting time or money.

The main sections are Home Tech, Cooking, Home & DIY, Buying Guides, and Tools & Calculators. Home Tech is divided into Wi-Fi & Routers, Gaming PCs, Laptops, Smart Home, Streaming & TVs, and Privacy & Security so related articles stay easy to find as the site grows.

How Biscuit is not a professional advice site. It is also not monetized in this build: no ads, affiliate links, sponsored posts, paid reviews, or product placements are wired in.
