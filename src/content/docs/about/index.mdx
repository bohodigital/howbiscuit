How Biscuit is a general-purpose explainer site for clear, answer-first articles across practical topics.

The site is organized around Math, Research & Writing, Cooking, Home Tech, Make-Do Lab, Tools, and supporting hubs for buying guides, everyday science, and glossary terms.

How Biscuit is not a professional advice site. It is also not monetized in this build: no ads, affiliate links, sponsored posts, paid reviews, or product placements are wired in.
