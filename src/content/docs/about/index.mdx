How Biscuit is an independent practical-guides publication operated by Boho Digital Services.

We publish answer-first guidance for people trying to fix something, choose a product, or understand a practical system without wasting time or money. The five public categories are Home Tech, Home & Apartment, Kitchen, Shop Smarter, and Tools.

This build has no affiliate links, sponsored placements, paid reviews, or product placements. Evidence and testing labels describe what was actually done; specification review is never presented as hands-on testing.
