## What it converts

Choose a measurement type, then choose the two units. US and Imperial liquid units are labeled separately. Decimal storage units and binary storage units are also kept separate.

Volume and weight are different measurements. A cup of flour and a cup of water do not weigh the same, so the converter does not invent an ingredient density.
