## What the estimate knows

Enter a street address and ZIP to load the state, county, city, and special-district sales-tax lines available in How Biscuit's public-source database. A ZIP-only lookup returns every matching jurisdiction when a postal area crosses tax boundaries, so the calculator does not silently guess which city applies.

If a verified local record is unavailable, the status message clearly switches to the state-only fallback. The state selector still covers all 50 states and Washington, DC, and every loaded rate remains editable.

Use the local fields when a receipt or official lookup gives the component rates. Use **I have a total budget** to answer the useful version of the question: how much sticker-price product fits inside the cash limit after checkout tax?

The NYC presets distinguish taxes added at checkout from excise already built into the shelf price. Adult-use cannabis uses a 9% state and 4% local retail tax and is exempt from general sales tax. NYC cigarettes show the $5.35 state and $1.50 city excise per pack as embedded rather than adding it twice. The grocery preset assumes unheated, unprepared food for home use; prepared food, candy, soft drinks, and alcohol can be taxable. Vapor products add New York's 20% supplemental tax to regular sales tax.

## Sources and limits

- General rates and tax boundaries for participating states: [Streamlined Sales Tax rate and boundary files](https://www.streamlinedsalestax.org/Shared-Pages/rate-and-boundary-files)
- Product taxability for participating states: [Streamlined Sales Tax state taxability matrices](https://www.streamlinedsalestax.org/Shared-Pages/State-taxability-matrix)
- Address normalization: [US Census Geocoding Services API](https://geocoding.geo.census.gov/geocoder/Geocoding_Services_API.html)
- Approximate postal autofill: [Cloudflare request location metadata](https://developers.cloudflare.com/workers/runtime-apis/request/#incomingrequestcfproperties)
- State-only postal fallback: [Zippopotam.us](https://api.zippopotam.us/), an Open Database License dataset
- State cigarette and vaping rules: [CDC STATE System datasets](https://data.cdc.gov/browse?category=Legislation)
- State alcohol, cannabis, cigarette, and vaping source tables: [Federation of Tax Administrators](https://taxadmin.org/tax-rates-new/)
- Non-participating states: [official state revenue agencies](https://www.streamlinedsalestax.org/contacts/state-contact-information)
- NYC general rate and ZIP warning: [New York Publication 718](https://www.tax.ny.gov/pdf/publications/sales/pub718.pdf)
- Adult-use cannabis: [New York State Department of Taxation and Finance](https://www.tax.ny.gov/bus/auc/)
- Cigarettes and prepaid tax: [New York State Department of Taxation and Finance](https://www.tax.ny.gov/bus/cig/cigidx.htm)
- Alcohol excise: [New York State Department of Taxation and Finance](https://www.tax.ny.gov/bus/bev/abt.htm)
- Food sold by food stores: [New York Tax Bulletin ST-283](https://www.tax.ny.gov/pubs_and_bulls/tg_bulletins/st/food_sold_by_food_stores.htm)
- Vapor products: [New York State Department of Taxation and Finance](https://www.tax.ny.gov/bus/vpt/)

The page sends the location to How Biscuit's same-origin gateway. Street addresses travel in the request body rather than the public URL. **Use my area** reads Cloudflare's approximate postal metadata and does not call a separate IP-location provider. The gateway checks our versioned public-source database first, then uses the Census Bureau for address normalization or Zippopotam for a ZIP-only fallback. Exact-address responses are not cached; ZIP-only jurisdiction results can be cached at the edge for up to 24 hours. Public providers may retain request logs. The calculator never requests your device location.

General and grocery rates can be loaded from machine-readable state files. Current cigarette excise can be itemized where the CDC publishes a directly computable per-pack amount. Vaping, alcohol, cannabis, and many local product rules use different units or legal conditions; the calculator shows those rules for review instead of inventing a checkout percentage. Verified presets and manual inputs remain available for those cases.

Special taxes change often and can depend on product type, potency, volume, seller, and exact location. Check a current official source before making a purchase or business decision.
