Research & Writing covers how to ask stronger questions, compare sources, structure explanations, and turn notes into usable answers.

This division should make the reasoning visible. When a claim depends on a source, link it. When a recommendation depends on judgment, state the tradeoff.

## Start Here

- Check whether a source exists before citing it.
- Keep assignment rules separate from general advice.
- Use AI for planning and revision support, not as a verified source.

## What This Section Covers

- source evaluation
- citations and outlines
- research notes
- academic-integrity boundaries
