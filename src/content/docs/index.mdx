<section class="hb-method-section" id="guide-standard">
  <div class="hb-method-lead">
    <span>OUR GUIDE STANDARD</span>
    <h2>Useful information in a predictable order.</h2>
  </div>
  <div class="hb-method-list">
    <div><b>1</b><span><strong>Answer first.</strong> State the likely solution or conclusion before the background.</span></div>
    <div><b>2</b><span><strong>Diagnose before changing things.</strong> Check the simplest likely causes in a sensible order.</span></div>
    <div><b>3</b><span><strong>Show the steps and expected result.</strong> Readers should know whether the fix worked.</span></div>
    <div><b>4</b><span><strong>State costs, tradeoffs, and stop points.</strong> Do not hide the part where professional help is safer or cheaper.</span></div>
  </div>
  <p>The goal is to help the reader solve the problem without creating a second one.</p>
</section>
