Home & DIY covers maintaining and improving a house or apartment, from routine cleaning and storage to repairs that require a clear professional stop point.

## Browse Home & DIY

<h2 id="repairs-maintenance">Repairs & Maintenance</h2>

Identify the cause before covering the symptom. Guides should list required tools and materials, realistic time and cost, expected service life, and how to verify the repair.

## Cleaning

Match the method to the material and contaminant. Explain dilution, ventilation, contact time, and incompatible chemicals without exaggerating ordinary household risks.

<h2 id="organization-storage">Organization & Storage</h2>

Prioritize access, frequency of use, available space, load limits, moisture, and fire safety. A system that requires constant re-sorting is not a practical system.

## Apartment Living

Focus on reversible improvements, landlord responsibilities, leases, documentation, noise, comfort, utilities, and avoiding damage charges.

<h2 id="tools-materials">Tools & Materials</h2>

Cover tool selection, safe use, maintenance, storage, consumables, and when renting or borrowing is better than buying.

<h2 id="safety-professional-help">Safety & Professional Help</h2>

Stop for live electrical work, gas, structural movement, uncontrolled water, sewage, significant mold, hazardous materials, or work prohibited by a lease, permit rule, or warranty.
