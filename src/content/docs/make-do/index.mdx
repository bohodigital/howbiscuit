Home & DIY covers the physical work of maintaining and improving a house or apartment: cleaning, storage, basic repairs, temporary fixes, and deciding when the owner, landlord, or a qualified professional needs to take over.

## Start Here

- Identify whether the problem is cosmetic, structural, electrical, plumbing-related, or a health hazard.
- Check the lease, warranty, and local responsibilities before altering a rented property.
- Prefer reversible repairs until the cause is known.
- Stop when the work involves live electricity, gas, structural damage, uncontrolled water, or a hazardous material.

## What This Section Covers

- apartment and basic home maintenance
- cleaning methods and material compatibility
- organization and storage
- low-cost and temporary repairs
- tools, materials, realistic costs, and professional stop points

Every guide should state what the repair can accomplish, how long it is likely to last, and what risk it does not solve.
