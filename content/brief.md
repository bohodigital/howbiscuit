# How Biscuit Brief

## Positioning

How Biscuit should be a broad explainer site with clean, useful answers across practical topics. It can test informational SEO patterns without putting a client site or the agency site at risk.

## Initial Content Pillars

- Simple science and everyday mechanics.
- Beginner-friendly explainers.
- Buying, fixing, and deciding guides.
- Curiosity-led posts with structured answers.
- SEO experiments around broad topical authority.

## Experiment Boundaries

This site can test broad topic clusters, answer-first introductions, featured-snippet formatting, schema variants, and internal linking across unrelated subject areas. Keep experiments documented in `experiments/registry.md`.
