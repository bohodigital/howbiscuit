# USDA data sources

## FoodData Central

Use bounded food search and detail operations. Store stable FDC identity and complete nutrient observations only. Do not infer missing nutrient labels or units. The 2026-07-24 starter release includes 15 reviewed staple-food identities and no incomplete nutrient rows.

## MyMarketNews

Use the report registry and bounded report sections. A report definition preserves market type and unit basis. The starter release covers Central U.S. butter, Central fluid milk and cream, shell-egg inventory, the national rice summary, and shipping-point methodology. Butter basis differentials and egg inventories are not consumer prices.

## NASS Quick Stats

Use bounded Quick Stats queries. The starter release contains 2021–2025 U.S. corn, rice, and wheat production. Units are bushels for corn and wheat and hundredweight for rice. Suppression markers remain null and are never estimated.

All credentials are injected by Local1’s server-managed broker. No provider key or bearer token is a repository or browser concern.
