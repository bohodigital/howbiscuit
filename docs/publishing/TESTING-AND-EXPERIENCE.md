# Testing and experience

Testing records state the tested subject, linked products, tester, dates, environment, method, measurements, observations, limitations, evidence artifacts, and claim state.

`hands-on-tested` and `owner-experience` require evidence artifacts and permit matching first-hand language. `not-hands-on-tested` and `not-applicable` do not. Product provenance must agree with article testing evidence and public prose; researched or listed-only records cannot claim use or testing, and listed-only records cannot recommend.

Never upgrade a claim state to make validation pass. Fix the prose or obtain real evidence.
