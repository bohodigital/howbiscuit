# Product groups

A product group declares its purpose, exact member product IDs, inclusion and exclusion criteria, methodology, evidence basis, source and testing IDs, recommendation state, review date or horizon, and status.

Membership is explicit and unique. Published groups may contain only published products. A best/ranking group needs a published recommendation claim evaluating exactly the same members with evidence and a review date.

Groups are static editorial records. They do not query retailers, geography, availability, or live prices.
