# Source policy

Register each source once under `content/sources/`. Record its title, publisher, author when known, canonical URL or publication identity, publication and access dates, source type, authority notes, archive notes, rights restrictions, and status.

Public citations must reference active source IDs declared by the article. A public source needs a canonical URL. Ordinary links remain subject to safe-URL validation; article commerce enforcement additionally requires raw absolute URLs to match a governed source or link preview.

Sources support claims; they do not grant media rights or testing provenance. Record those separately.
