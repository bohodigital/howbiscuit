# Shopping links and disclosures

Handoff 2 may render only explicit unpaid merchant destinations for an exact product variant. Destination URLs must be credential-free HTTPS URLs with no query string or fragment. Every rendered link states that How Biscuit receives no compensation and includes its capture date.

Price claims are structured, dated observations with currency, product, source or destination, context, and review date. Raw prices, live availability, local availability, arbitrary merchant URLs, and unsupported rankings in article prose fail closed.

Future affiliate records may be staged but cannot be published. Affiliate activation, click analytics, live offers, and retailer adapters belong exclusively to Handoff 3.
